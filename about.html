ABOUT PROJECT

This is the Utility Data Portal for Ward 2 of the Annapurna Rural Municipality. It includes all types of utility data and their visualization. Through this portal, users can query and communicate problems related to utilities like electricity, water supply, roads, internet, drainage, etc. Our main goal is to make a web-based GIS data portal to improve the ward's utility management system. And this is the pilot project carried out by the students of the Department of Geomatics Engineering, Pashchimanchal Campus.
